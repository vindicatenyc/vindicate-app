# YouTube Short Script Template

**Duration:** 60 seconds max
**Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical)

## Structure

### HOOK (0-3 seconds)
- Attention-grabbing question or stat
- Examples:
  - "Did you know debt collectors violate the law 79% of the time?"
  - "What if I told you there's a formula to settle your tax debt?"

### CONTEXT (3-15 seconds)
- What is Vindicate NYC
- "We're building open-source tools to help you fight back"

### DEMO (15-45 seconds)
- Screen recording of feature in action
- Voice over explaining each step
- Keep it visual, minimize text

### CTA (45-60 seconds)
- "Link in bio to try it free"
- "Subscribe for more legal tech tips"
- "Comment what feature you want next"
